what is biotechnology ? 12 Feb 2024

Biotechnology is a field that merges biology and technology to create innovative solutions for healthcare, agriculture, industry, and the environment. It uses living organisms, cells, and biological systems to develop new products and processes. Key areas include medical biotechnology (e.g., drug development, gene therapy), agricultural biotechnology (e.g., GMOs, biofertilizers), industrial biotechnology (e.g., biofuels, bioplastics), and environmental biotechnology (e.g., bioremediation). Biotechnology has significantly advanced medicine, improved food production, and provided sustainable environmental solutions.
